Empowering Comfort and Independence

Our AssistBot is designed with the user in mind, offering a range of intuitive functions that enhance daily living. Below are the key features that make our device an essential companion for users seeking comfort and independence.

1. Assistance at the Push of a Button

For Users: Simply press Button A on your AssistBot to request immediate assistance. This action sends a signal to your helper’s device, ensuring you receive the help you need without delay.

For Helpers: When the signal is received, a cheerful alert sound plays, and the LED display lights up with the message 'HELP'. Your prompt acknowledgment by pressing 'A' will reassure the user that help is on the way.

2. Smart Fall Detection

For Users: Equipped with an advanced accelerometer, the AssistBot automatically detects if you have fallen. It will send an alert signal to your helper’s device, continuously beeping until assistance arrives.

For Helpers: Upon receiving the alert, the device plays a distinctive sound and displays 'FALL'. You can acknowledge the signal by pressing the reset button, ensuring the user gets the timely support they need.

3. Vitamin Consumption Reminder

For Users: Maintain your health effortlessly! When it’s time to take your vitamins, simply press Button B. The AssistBot will display the type and quantity of vitamins you need, helping you manage your health independently.

This feature is especially beneficial for users with memory challenges, providing a gentle reminder that supports autonomy.

4. Hydration Reminder Before Bed

For Users: As you prepare for sleep, the AssistBot detects low light levels and gently reminds you to hydrate by displaying 'DRINK' along with a visual icon. Stay healthy and hydrated even as you wind down for the night!

Understanding Our Sensors

The AssistBot incorporates advanced sensors to ensure reliable performance and user safety. Here’s how each sensor contributes to the overall functionality:

1. Accelerometer

Detects free fall conditions to trigger alerts to the helper.

2. Light Sensor

Senses low light to remind the user to drink water before sleeping.

3. Radio Sensor

Facilitates communication between the user and helper’s AssistBot for status updates and alerts.

Empowering users for a more comfortable and independent life!
